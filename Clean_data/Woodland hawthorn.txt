Citation
Antidiabetics
Hawthorn does not appear to affect the glycaemic control in patients taking conventional antidiabetic drugs.
Walker AF, Marakis G, Simpson E, Hope JL, Robinson PA, Hassanein M, Simpson HCR. Hypotensive effects of hawthorn for patients with diabetes taking prescription drugs: a randomised controlled trial. Br J Gen Pract (2006) 56, 437–43.
Antihypertensives
Limited evidence suggests that there may be additive blood pressure-lowering effects if hawthorn is taken with conventional antihypertensives, but the effects are small.
Walker AF, Marakis G, Simpson E, Hope JL, Robinson PA, Hassanein M, Simpson HCR. Hypotensive effects of hawthorn for patients with diabetes taking prescription drugs: a randomised controlled trial. Br J Gen Pract (2006) 56, 437–43.
Digoxin
Hawthorn does not appear to affect digoxin levels.
Tankanow R, Tamer HR, Streetman DS, Smith SG, Welton JL, Annesley T, Aaronson KD, Bleske BE. Interaction study between digoxin and a preparation of hawthorn (Crataegus oxyacantha). J Clin Pharmacol (2003) 43, 637–42.
