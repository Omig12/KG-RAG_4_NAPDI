Citation
Antiplatelet drugs
Evening primrose oil can inhibit platelet aggregation and increase bleeding time. It has therefore been suggested that it may have additive effects with other antiplatelet drugs, but evidence of this is generally lacking.
Guivernau M, Meza N, Barja P, Roman O. Clinical and experimental study on the longterm effect of dietary gamma-linolenic acid on plasma lipids, platelet aggregation, thromboxane formation, and prostacyclin production. Prostaglandins Leukot Essent Fatty Acids (1994) 51, 311–16.
